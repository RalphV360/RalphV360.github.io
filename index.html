GETTING STARTED WITH PHOTOGRAPHY

WHAT IS PHOTOGRAPHY?

Photography is the practice of capturing images using a camera or similar device. It has become an important medium for visual communication and artistic expression, allowing people to capture moments and memories, create art, document historical events, and tell stories. Photography can take many forms and can be used to create a variety of moods and effects through different techniques, lighting, and camera settings. With the rise of digital cameras and smartphones, photography has become more accessible than ever before, making it easy for people to capture and share their photos with others through social media and online platforms.
